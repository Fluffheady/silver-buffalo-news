Why We Started Silver Buffalo News

Every week in Western New York, thousands of seniors miss out on programs, benefits, and money that they're fully entitled to — simply because no one told them about it in plain English.

HEAP heating assistance. Enhanced STAR property tax exemptions. The EPIC pharmaceutical program. Veterans benefits that go unclaimed for years. Medicare options that could save hundreds a month. These programs exist, they work, and Erie County residents qualify for them. But local news coverage of the 55+ community in Buffalo has been gutted over the past decade — and that gap costs real people real money.

Silver Buffalo News exists to close that gap. We cover five beats that matter most to our community: Health & Longevity, Local Money & Benefits, Consumer Rights, Nostalgia & Collectibles, and DIY & Home. Every story we publish is grounded in real WNY context — not generic national content repurposed for a local audience.

We are committed to clear writing, large type, and content that respects our readers' intelligence. If we write about a benefit program, we tell you exactly how to apply, what documents to bring, and what the deadline is. No vague summaries. No paywalls. Just useful information, written for Buffalo.

Our editorial standard: Every article we publish goes through a fact-checking process. When we cite a government program, we link to the primary source. We do not accept payment for editorial coverage. Advertising is clearly labeled.

Our Editorial Standards

1

We Cite Our Sources

Every claim we make about a government program, health study, or benefit includes a link to the primary source. We don't ask you to take our word for it.

2

Advertising is Always Labeled

Paid advertising appears in clearly marked zones. We do not accept payment for editorial coverage — ever. Our coverage is not for sale.

3

We Update When Things Change

Program deadlines change. Benefit amounts change. When we learn something we published is out of date, we update it — and note the update at the top of the article.

4

We Are Not Financial or Medical Advisors

Our articles are for informational purposes only. For financial, legal, or medical decisions, always consult a qualified professional. We tell you what to look for — then help you find the right person to call.

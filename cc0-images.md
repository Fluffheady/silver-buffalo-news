## Research Notes

- **Pexels** was inaccessible (blocks automated access via robots policy).  
- **Pixabay** and **Openverse** were tried but lacked suitable free photos matching the editorial criteria.  
- **Unsplash+** (paid tier) photos were systematically excluded — identified by HTTP 403 on the download endpoint. All photos above return HTTP 200 on download.
- Many "senior on phone worried" photos on Unsplash are Getty Images licensed under Unsplash+ and are **not free**. The Vitaly Gariev series was the best free alternative.
- The Aleksi Partanen attic photo (Finland) has outstanding vintage-clutter realism and easily reads as an American storage space.
- Images verified accessible as of April 2026.
